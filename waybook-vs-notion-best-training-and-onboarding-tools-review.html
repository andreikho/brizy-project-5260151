Waybook: User Interface & User Experience (UI / UX)

When you log into your Waybook, you’ll find a visually appealing dashboard, your workspace, a search bar, a list of documents, and three menus that explain everything:

(1) You can assign some functions to track progress and resend activities related to documents and improvements. On the top you can find the search tap.

(2) The second menu, at the top, takes you to your main dashboard, including Waybook, Members, Analytics, and Settings.

(3) The third menu contains the knowledge base, live chat, and the profile settings.

(3) When you work on a document, there is a menu you can access to change the settings, such as sharing it with someone via a public link.

Notion: User Interface & User Experience (UI / UX)

Notion also has similar menus; let us clarify:

(1) You can manage search, track all current activity in the workspace, change settings — integrations, billing branding, and member information.

(2) You can access templates, import from other tools we will see, and use the dustbin if you have deleted something and want to restore it.

(3) The third menu lets you share the document you are working on, track the record’s update, like a log, and set other options for it.

Pricing

Waybook Pricing Overview

Waybook has a simple pricing plan: $99/month and 20 users included. So if we do the math, it's less than $5 per user for the first twenty seats and another $5 per month for an additional seat.

If you can pay annually, you get a discount of almost 20%, bringing the monthly cost down to $83/month for the first 20 seats and $4.17 for each additional seat. Every penny counts. 💸

Prices Waybook| Waybook vs Notion Review | Best Onboarding Tool

Notion Pricing Overview

Notion charges you a monthly fee of $10 per member or $96 per year per seat (Team Plan). For example, if you pay per month and have 20 members in your Waybook, you pay double that, or about $200 per month.

You can also expect a 20% discount if you choose to pay annually, which is $160, so each member can cost $8.

Asking Price | Waybook vs Notion Review

Conclusion and Price Summary:

  • Both offer a free trial, and Notion has a free personal plan forever (Limited and No for a team).
  • Opting for an annual payment will reduce both by around 20%, but it depends on you.
  • With twenty seats, you have more room to grow your team for less money with Waybook.
  • Waybook is more affordable per seat and has a very competitive price among similar tools.
  • Both tools have scalable strategy pricing, but still, Waybook has the better price overall.

Pricing Evaluation.

Comparing the same plan (Team), Waybook is more accessible with more valuable features to offer training and onboarding system. It is therefore a no-brainer.
